Skills Development

NSS focuses on developing various skills among students, which can significantly contribute to women entrepreneurship. By providing training and awareness programs tailored to women's needs, NSS empowers women to become successful entrepreneurs. Through skill-building activities such as workshops, seminars, and practical training sessions, NSS equips women with the necessary knowledge and expertise to start and manage their businesses effectively.
